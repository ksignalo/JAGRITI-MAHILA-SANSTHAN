
5. Impact / Achievements
Impact & Achievements
● Over the past three decades, Mahila Jagriti Sansthan (MJS) has been working
at the grassroots level to empower marginalized communities, particularly
women and children. Through community participation, skill development
initiatives, child protection programs, and awareness campaigns, the
organization has created meaningful and sustainable impact across several
districts of Bihar.
● Our work reflects a long-standing commitment to social justice, gender equality,
and community empowerment. The following highlights demonstrate the impact
of our initiatives over the years.
30+ Years of Grassroots Service
● Since its establishment in 1993, Mahila Jagriti Sansthan has been continuously
working to uplift marginalized communities. With more than three decades of
experience, the organization has developed strong relationships with
communities and institutions, enabling it to design and implement effective
grassroots development programs.
● Through consistent engagement and community participation, MJS has built trust
and credibility among rural communities and continues to play an active role in
addressing social challenges related to women empowerment, education, and
child protection.
Empowering 15,000+ Women through Skill Development and Livelihood
Programs
● One of the major achievements of Mahila Jagriti Sansthan has been its
contribution to women’s empowerment. Through skill development training,
entrepreneurship support, and Self-Help Group (SHG) initiatives, the
organization has empowered more than 15,000 women over the years.
● Training programs in areas such as cutting and stitching, jute bag making,
handicrafts, and other livelihood activities have helped women develop
practical skills and become economically independent. Many women trained
through these programs have been able to start small businesses or contribute to
their family’s income, thereby improving their social and economic status.
Rescuing and Protecting 3,500+ Children from Child Labour
● Mahila Jagriti Sansthan has played a significant role in child protection and
child labour eradication efforts. In collaboration with organizations such as
Bachpan Bachao Andolan, the organization has contributed to rescuing and
rehabilitating more than 3,500 children from situations of child labour,
exploitation, and trafficking.
● Through community awareness campaigns, child rights advocacy, and initiatives
like Baal Panchayat and Baal Sansad, the organization has worked to promote
children’s participation and ensure that children have access to education and a
safe environment.
Promoting Education for Children from Marginalized Communities
● Education has been a central focus of Mahila Jagriti Sansthan’s work. The
organization has supported children’s education through various initiatives
including Utpreran Kendra learning centers, awareness programs, and
distribution of educational materials such as books, notebooks, and
stationery.
● Additionally, with the support of partner organizations, the organization provides
free virtual educational support for children from Class 1 to Class 5, helping
them strengthen their foundational learning and academic performance.
● These initiatives aim to encourage school enrollment, reduce dropout rates, and
ensure that children from disadvantaged backgrounds receive the support they
need to continue their education.
Community Development Across Multiple Districts of Bihar
● Mahila Jagriti Sansthan has implemented development programs across several
districts of Bihar including Darbhanga, Madhubani, Saharsa, Supaul,
Muzaffarpur, Motihari, Bhojpur, Buxar, Nawada, and Patna.
● Through partnerships with government agencies, civil society organizations, and
local communities, the organization has conducted numerous programs related
to women empowerment, child protection, environmental awareness, health
education, skill development, and disaster relief.
● These initiatives have positively impacted thousands of families and contributed
to the overall development of rural communities.
Disaster Relief and Emergency Support
● Mahila Jagriti Sansthan has also played an active role in supporting communities
during emergencies. During natural disasters such as floods and crises such as
the COVID-19 pandemic, the organization provided essential relief support
including food supplies, masks, sanitizers, and educational materials to
vulnerable families.
● These efforts helped communities cope with immediate challenges while
reinforcing the organization’s commitment to humanitarian assistance.
Continuing the Journey of Social Change
● With more than 30 years of experience and a strong grassroots presence,
Mahila Jagriti Sansthan continues to work toward empowering women, protecting
children’s rights, and promoting sustainable community development.
● The organization remains committed to expanding its impact through
partnerships, innovative programs, and community participation, striving to build
a society where every individual has the opportunity to live with dignity, equality,
and hope.
